How to Use a Self-Service Car Wash: Washing, Rinsing, & Drying

STEP 1: Pull into an empty bay and park your car

STEP 2: Hang your floor mats on the provided metal clips

STEP 3: Insert coins or a credit card into the machine

STEP 4: Set the dial to “Wash” or “Pre-Soak”

STEP 5: Stand about 3 to 5 feet (0.91 to 1.52 m) away from your car as you wash

STEP 6: Rinse your entire car with water and Apply soap to your car with the spray wand

STEP 7: Rinse the foam brush with the spray wand

STEP 8: Apply tire cleaner to the wheels and tires
